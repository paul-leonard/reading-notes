### Class 39: DSA Review

**Reading Material (or Exercise) for the Assignment**
1. [Whiteboard Advice](https://hackernoon.com/the-best-whiteboard-interview-advice-i-ever-received-3ebbfa72e4a)
- the biggest thing is communciation
- it shows off what it is like to work with you and your level of understanding
- restate the question
- ask clarifying questions including about edge cases
- ask about testing and verification cases
- ask the interviewer if the psedudocode makes sense to them
- then get to actually writing the code
- if you get stuck, casually ask for a little help or a nudge
- ask questions about the technical interview before the interview
- we're all human and the interviewer probably has compassion for what you are going through



1. [6 Tips](https://blog.usejournal.com/6-tips-to-ace-a-whiteboard-programming-interview-f06c1b378bc6)
- actually a full 7 tips!  (7 minute abs!)
- take a few minutes to solve the problem yourself before talking through it: "If it is okay with you, I'd like to take a few moments..."; "So my conclusions are ..... I think the general steps involved in reaching the conclusion are..."
- write out the solution steps
- write pseudocode before real code
- "don't sweat the small stuff": use equivalents; describe what it is you would look up to find the syntax
- "sit down. be humble.": be the good person you are
- prepare and study
- save time to review your work and solution to check for errors
